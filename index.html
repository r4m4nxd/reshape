THE NON-NEGOTIABLES
1
Sleep 7.5–8 hrs nightly
Poor sleep raises cortisol → stores belly fat. Bed by 10pm on weekdays.
2
10,000 steps every day

3
Never miss 2 workouts in a row
Missing one is human. Missing two starts a quitting habit.

No refined carbs or sugar — ever

6
Respect the PCL — be patient
